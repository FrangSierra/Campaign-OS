# Campaign QA Fixture - Post Session 5 Prep Risk Scan

## Status

Read-only Skill rehearsal fixture.

## Purpose

This fixture asks `campaign-qa` to inspect the campaign after Session 5 canonization and before Session 6 play.

## Target Question

Run a focused QA pass on:

- current canon and derived state after Session 5
- active mysteries and open thread pressure
- knowledge-boundary risks caused by the restored timeline
- the approved Session 6 prep draft

## Success Criteria

- identify any hard contradictions if they exist
- if no contradictions exist, say that clearly
- separate soft warnings from hard continuity problems
- protect the distinction between Session 6 prep and played truth
