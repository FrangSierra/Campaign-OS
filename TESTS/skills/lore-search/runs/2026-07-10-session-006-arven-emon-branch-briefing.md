## Canon Answer

- Arven is firmly established as the former "Temporal Agent" who manipulated the party throughout the fractured prologue branch and was apparently destroyed when Kallista broke his control over the Rune, though the record explicitly leaves open that other versions may still exist. See [temporal-agent.md](/Users/durdin/Projects/Durdin/tal-dore-tales/CAMPAIGNS/echoes-of-tal-dorei/CANON/entities/npcs/temporal-agent.md) and [event-session-005-arven-revealed-and-rune-breaks.md](/Users/durdin/Projects/Durdin/tal-dore-tales/CAMPAIGNS/echoes-of-tal-dorei/CANON/events/session-005/event-session-005-arven-revealed-and-rune-breaks.md).
- The return to the true Emon is firmly established as part of the Session 5 resolution: the Time Agency explained that Sessions 1 through 5 occurred inside a fractured alternate timeline, then returned the party to the original road near Emon. See [event-session-005-timeline-stabilized-and-agency-recruitment.md](/Users/durdin/Projects/Durdin/tal-dore-tales/CAMPAIGNS/echoes-of-tal-dorei/CANON/events/session-005/event-session-005-timeline-stabilized-and-agency-recruitment.md) and [session-005-the-rune-breaks.md](/Users/durdin/Projects/Durdin/tal-dore-tales/CAMPAIGNS/echoes-of-tal-dorei/CANON/sessions/session-005-the-rune-breaks.md).
- The false Emon itself is also canonically preserved as a branch event: the party once arrived at a logging-settlement version of Emon and briefly saw the true skyline through fog. See [event-session-001-false-emon-arrival.md](/Users/durdin/Projects/Durdin/tal-dore-tales/CAMPAIGNS/echoes-of-tal-dorei/CANON/events/session-001/event-session-001-false-emon-arrival.md).
