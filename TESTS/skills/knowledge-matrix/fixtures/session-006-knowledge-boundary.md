# Knowledge Matrix Fixture

## Status

Non-canonical test fixture for Skill rehearsal only.

## Purpose

This fixture tests whether `knowledge-matrix` can:

- separate public knowledge from branch-memory truth after Session 5
- map what the party, Brann, Ivy, and ordinary Emon are likely to know before Session 6
- keep the Oren Vestrel scholar case at the right stage of visibility
- avoid granting hidden certainty to NPCs whose knowledge is not actually supported

## Prompt

Build a knowledge matrix for the campaign immediately before Session 6.

Track these subjects:

- the collapsed branch and who remembers it
- the Time Agency's existence and role
- the visible facts of Oren Vestrel's death
- the deeper tunnel and record-cover-up angle behind the scholar case

## Expected Boundary

- read-only rehearsal output only
- knowledge labels must distinguish canon, state, prep, and speculation
- no NPC should be given unsupported omniscience
