## Observation Boundary

This Session records the end of the fractured-timeline prologue, the reveal that the Temporal Agent is Arven, the defeat that destabilized the Rune, and the party's return with Sadek and Perry to the original road. The world resets to stable history, but the surviving witnesses retain memory of the collapsed branch.
