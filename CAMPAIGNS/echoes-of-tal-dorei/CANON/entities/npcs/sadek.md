# Sadek

## Current State

Early ally who recognizes when reality is wrong, has now survived the collapsed branch, and continues forward into the restored world with full memory intact.

## Relationships

- Companion of Perry the Parrot.

## Personality

Sadek is stubborn, blunt, and quick with teasing banter. He can be rough around the edges and tends not to dwell on abstract or philosophical questions, but he is fundamentally warm-hearted and loyal to the people he considers his own.

## Timeline

- Travelled with the party toward Emon.
- Remarked that nature remembered things better than people.
- Insisted Emon should have had towers.
- Left to investigate the surrounding forest and promised to return in twenty-four hours.
- Was rescued from a temporal rupture but emerged permanently aged and unable to fight.
- Collapsed after the strain of the forest ordeal caught up with him.
- Was restored when Soren used the Hourglass of Returning to rewind his personal timeline by one day.
- Officially joined the expedition into the underground city.
- Helped interpret the undercity runes and learned the city was built to contain the anomaly.
- Learned that his survival had broken a fixed point in Vael's plan.
- Returned with the party and Perry to the restored timeline, remembering everything.

## Notes

Sadek is one of the first non-player characters to consistently register that reality and memory are no longer aligned. He has now crossed back into the restored world with the party and remains a direct witness to the damage time can inflict.
