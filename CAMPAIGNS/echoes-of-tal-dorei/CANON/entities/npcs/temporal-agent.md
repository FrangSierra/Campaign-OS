# Arven

## Current State

Historical manipulator of the fractured prologue branch, first encountered under the false identity of the Temporal Agent.

## Relationships

No canonical relationship has been approved yet.

## Timeline

- Appeared when time froze inside the Mage Tower under the false identity of the Temporal Agent.
- Warned that reality is collapsing and pointed the party toward the forest ruins.
- Was not freed by the party at the tower stage.
- Became a stronger object of suspicion after the party found evidence of deliberate Rune study and timeline selection ideology.
- Was revealed as Arven after Vael's mask shattered and the Rune crisis escalated.
- Was apparently destroyed in that branch when Kallista broke his control, though he implied that other versions may remain.

## Notes

Arven remains important enough to preserve as a canonical Entity even though Vael stays session-level only and other one-scene NPCs from the prologue were intentionally not retained as standalone Entity records.
