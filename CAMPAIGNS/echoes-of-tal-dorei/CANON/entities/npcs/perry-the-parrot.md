# Perry the Parrot

## Current State

Talking parrot who often says things that sound absurd until later evidence suggests they were warning signs, and who now remains with Sadek and the party in the restored timeline.

## Relationships

- Companion of Sadek.

## Timeline

- Travelled with the party toward Emon.
- Made odd comments during the first road anomalies.
- Shouted phrases like "Wrong road!" and "Where did the city go?"
- Cried for help as the party left the Mage Tower.
- Led the party to Sadek in the forest and warned, "He tried three times."
- Stayed with Sadek and the party after Sadek's restoration at the threshold of the underground city.
- Commented uneasily throughout the undercity while continuing to reveal useful clues.
- Returned with Sadek and the party to the restored timeline, remembering everything.

## Notes

Perry remains comic relief on the surface, but canon now supports treating his remarks as recurring signals that something is wrong with the timeline.
