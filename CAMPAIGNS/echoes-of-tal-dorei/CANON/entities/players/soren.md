# Soren

## Current State

Cleric who has shifted from Sarenrae to Shar, saved Sadek, preserved Kallista from erasure, and now carries both the cost and the memory of the collapsed prologue branch into the real timeline.

## Relationships

- Research partnership with Phirina.

## Timeline

- Joined the caravan to Emon at Emerald Outpost.
- Witnessed the fog discontinuity and the false arrival at Emon.
- Investigated the sudden market and the map-seller clues with Phirina.
- Entered the Mage Tower and agreed to investigate the forest ruins.
- Accepted Shar's bargain during the trial of compassion.
- Lost the emotions tied to one defining childhood memory while retaining the memory itself.
- Helped defeat the Stone Keeper of Judgment.
- Used the Hourglass of Returning to save Sadek's life.
- Reached the entrance of the forgotten underground city.
- Crossed the undercity and learned Sadek's survival had broken Vael's fixed-point calculations.
- Received Shar's final miracle to prevent Kallista from being erased during the Paradox Wyrm battle.
- Returned to the restored timeline and accepted future work with the Time Agency.
