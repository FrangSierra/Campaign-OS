  Session 1 - The Road to Emon (Summary)

  Purpose
  Introduce the party, establish Emon as the greatest city in Tal'Dorei, and slowly reveal that reality has been rewritten.

  Major Events
  The party meets at Emerald Outpost and joins a caravan heading to Emon.
  They travel alongside Garrick Thornwall, Mira, Finn, Sadek Bronzebeard, his parrot Perry, and the elderly Elira Voss.
  Garrick constantly praises Emon, mentioning that if it hadn't been for his wife, he would have remained a drunk wasting his life in the city.
  During the journey, subtle reality glitches occur:
  A brief rewind noticed by only one character.
  Repeated animals and sounds.
  Missing tracks.
  Elira occasionally forgets previous conversations and says things like "I remember this differently."
  Sadek remarks that nature feels wrong, saying "The forest remembers things better than people do."
  Perry provides comic relief, occasionally blurting out odd comments that hint something is wrong, though everyone dismisses him as a silly bird.

  Climax
  Near Emon, the caravan passes through a dense supernatural fog.
  After clearing a fallen tree and returning:
  Mira and Finn have completely disappeared.
  Their belongings are gone.
  Garrick is now drunk, bitter, and aggressive, insisting he never had a wife or child, behaving like the alcoholic he once claimed he would have become without them.
  The fog clears to reveal that Emon no longer exists.
  In its place stands a muddy logging settlement that everyone insists has always been there.
  Nobody besides the party questions this version of history.

  Important NPCs
  Garrick Thornwall - Caravan master whose entire life is rewritten after the fog.
  Sadek Bronzebeard - Dwarf hunter who vaguely remembers that something is wrong with reality and may become an ally.
  Perry - Sadek's talking parrot and comic relief, occasionally saying unsettling things without realizing their importance.
  Elira Voss - Elderly woman who seems partially aware of the instability of reality.

  Evidence Found
  After arriving at the logging settlement, Elira Voss has disappeared, leaving behind only a small bag containing a mysterious key.

  Ending
  For a brief moment through the fog, the party catches a glimpse of the true skyline of Emon before it vanishes again, confirming that reality itself has somehow been altered rather than the city simply being destroyed.

  Character Notes
  Helmut
  A shy but kind-hearted Paladin travelling to Emon in search of work.
  Uses his helmet as a psychological persona: when he feels afraid or lacks confidence, he puts it on and becomes fearless and decisive.
  The helmet is not magical; it is entirely a roleplay aspect of his personality.

  Soren
  A cleric devoted to Sarenrae, introduced in this setting as the Goddess of Light.
  Travels to Emon to visit churches and speak with other priests.
  Has Tourette syndrome.
  Strongly defends the sick, minorities, and anyone treated as an outcast or "freak," becoming visibly upset by discrimination.

  Kallista
  Travels to Emon looking for work and wealth.
  Highly motivated by money and financial opportunities, often making decisions based on potential profit.

  Phirina
  A scholar and mage travelling to Emon to meet other scholars and expand her magical knowledge.
  Fascinated by magic to the point of becoming completely absorbed in it, frequently losing track of time while studying or experimenting.
