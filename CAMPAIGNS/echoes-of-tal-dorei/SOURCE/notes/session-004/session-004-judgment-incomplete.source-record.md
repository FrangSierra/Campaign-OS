  Session 4 – Judgment Incomplete (Summary)

  Purpose
  Conclude the Keepers' trials, defeat the Stone Guardian, and open the path toward the ancient underground city where the source of the temporal anomalies awaits.

  Stone Keeper of Judgment
  The session begins immediately with the battle against the Stone Keeper of Judgment.
  Because the party completed three of the four trials, only three of the guardian's six arms remain active while the others hang broken from its body.
  During the fight, the statue's damaged body begins collapsing, briefly animating its shattered arms before making one final stand.
  After being defeated, the guardian kneels and speaks its final words:
  "Judgment... Incomplete."
  It crumbles into rubble, revealing a Stone Key.
  The battle serves as the party's first major boss encounter and confirms that the Keepers' trials were designed to measure character rather than simply strength.

  Level Up
  After defeating the guardian, the party reaches Level 3.
  The group takes time to:
  - Discuss the powerful relics recovered from the Mage Tower.
  - Reflect on Soren's recent pact with Shar.
  - Rest before continuing deeper into the ruins.

  The Ancient Gate
  Using the Stone Key, the enormous gate hidden within the ruins finally opens.
  Rather than revealing another corridor or mine, the party discovers an entire forgotten underground city, filled with colossal dwarven and elven architecture, massive bridges, towering pillars, and ruins stretching far beyond what anyone expected.
  The source of the anomaly lies somewhere below.

  Saving Sadek
  The effort of crossing the forest and surviving the temporal anomaly finally catches up with Sadek Bronzebeard.
  Shortly after the battle, he collapses, barely breathing.
  The party debates whether to use one of their most valuable treasures:
  The Hourglass of Returning, a legendary single-use artifact capable of rewinding a person's personal timeline by 24 hours.
  Phirina argues against spending such a powerful item so early in the campaign.
  Before the discussion can continue, Soren, who is carrying the Hourglass, silently decides that Sadek's life is worth more than the artifact.
  He activates the Hourglass.
  Sadek's personal timeline rewinds by one day, restoring his body to the condition it was in before the catastrophic aging caused by the forest.
  The legendary item is consumed.
  Grateful for being given a second chance, Sadek officially joins the expedition and prepares a hearty dinner for the party before everyone settles in for a well-earned long rest.

  Ending
  The party camps at the entrance to the ancient underground city, fully aware that they are about to descend into the place where the temporal catastrophe truly began.
  Session 5 will begin with their exploration of the forgotten mines and the ancient city beneath the mountain.
