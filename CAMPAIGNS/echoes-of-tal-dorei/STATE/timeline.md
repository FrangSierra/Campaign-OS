# Timeline

- Session 1: The party met at Emerald Outpost and joined a caravan heading to Emon.
- Session 1: Road anomalies signaled that memory and reality were already unstable.
- Session 1: A supernatural fog erased the expected continuity around Garrick, Mira, and Finn.
- Session 1: The party arrived at a logging-settlement version of Emon and briefly glimpsed the true skyline through the fog.
- Session 2: The settlement continued to behave like the accepted version of Emon while alternate continuity surfaced around the party.
- Session 2: Jarek gifted the Living Map and the Tree coin, advancing the constellation puzzle.
- Session 2: The Laughing Lamia and its rooms suggested alternate versions of the party already existed in this reality.
- Session 2: Phirina solved the symbol puzzle and manifested the Mage Tower.
- Session 2: The Temporal Agent identified the forest ruins as the original breach site, and the party agreed to investigate.
- Session 3: The party found Sadek in the broken-time forest, rescued him, and saw him become permanently aged and unable to fight.
- Session 3: The Keepers' trials tested strength, courage, compassion, and knowledge as measures of character.
- Session 3: Soren accepted Shar's bargain and permanently shifted away from Sarenrae.
- Session 3: Hidden evidence in the temporal hideout showed that the Rune had been studied intentionally by someone who wanted only one timeline to survive.
- Session 3: The six-armed stone guardian awakened as the session ended.
- Session 4: The party defeated the Stone Keeper of Judgment and recovered the Stone Key.
- Session 4: The ancient gate opened onto a vast underground city beneath the mountain.
- Session 4: Soren consumed the Hourglass of Returning to restore Sadek's body and save his life.
- Session 4: The party rested at the threshold of the underground city before descending further.
- Session 5: The party crossed the undercity and learned it was built to contain the anomaly, not worship it.
- Session 5: Vael Thamior was confronted as the exhausted Keeper-Archivist behind the correction process.
- Session 5: The Temporal Agent was revealed as Arven, and the Rune crisis escalated into the Paradox Wyrm battle.
- Session 5: The party survived collapse through their relics, with Kallista breaking Arven's control.
- Session 5: The Time Agency explained the fractured prologue branch, and the party, Sadek, and Perry returned to the true road to Emon remembering everything.
